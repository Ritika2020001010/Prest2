Phase One of Prestige Raintree Park features 1368 units across 18 towers, each comprising 21 floors (2 basements, ground, plus 19 stories). The project offers a diverse range of apartments, including 3-bedroom units ranging from 2005 to 2020 square feet, 3-bedroom units with maid’s quarters between 2187 and 2400 square feet, and 3-bedroom units with a study and maid’s quarters from 2451 to 2588 square feet. Additionally, there are larger configurations available such as 4-bedroom units with maid's quarters ranging from 2900 to 2956 square feet, and 4-bedroom units with an additional studio (effectively 5 bedrooms) ranging from 3500 to 3700 square feet.

About Us

Prestige Raintree Park is a Classic, Cutting Edge, mixed-use township in Varthur, Whitefield Road, Bangalore. Spread over 107 acres and comprises 82 acres of the Residential complex and 25 acres of commercial space. Eighteen high-rise towers hosting 1368 apartment units in 3, 3.5, and 4 BHK configurations.
